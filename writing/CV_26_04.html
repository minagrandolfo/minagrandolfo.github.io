Doctoral research

PhD candidate — Lucid dreaming, interoception & multisensory integration
Center for Research in Cognition & Neurosciences (CRCN), Université Libre de Bruxelles
2023 — present Brussels, Belgium
  • Experiment 1 — Cardio-visual enfacement illusion (n = 47; 22 fLD / 25 nLD). Manuscript in preparation.
