## RESEARCH EXPERIENCE

**Doctoral research — Lucid dreaming, interoception & multisensory integration** — *2023 to present*
CRCN, Université Libre de Bruxelles

- **Experiment 1** — Cardio-visual enfacement illusion (n = 47; 22 fLD, 25 nLD). Manuscript in preparation.
- **Experiment 2** — Heartbeat Discrimination Task (n = 53). Interoceptive metacognition via HMeta-d. Data collection ongoing; manuscript in preparation.
- **Experiment 3** — Rubber Hand Illusion (n > 55). Proprioceptive drift and body ownership in lucid vs. non-lucid dreamers; no group difference at current sample size.
- **Experiment 4** — VR Rod-and-Frame task (data collection started April 2026; pilots n = 10, target n = 45). Bayesian visuo-vestibular integration (Alberts 2016) with adaptive Psi method (Prins 2013 / Legrand 2022). Pre-registration planned.

Ongoing collaboration with **Audrey Mazancieux** (Université Grenoble-Alpes) on a joint literature review on metacognition and lucid dreaming (summer 2026).
